# Overview
TenderBot democratizes access to UK public sector tenders by using AI to discover, summarise, and deliver the most relevant opportunities to SMEs. It offers the power of high-end tender intelligence platforms like Tenders Direct or Contracts Advance, but at less than 10% of the cost—fully automated, self-service, and simple to use. 

Primary Goal: Automate the discovery and delivery of relevant public sector opportunities through real-time tender data ingestion, AI summarisation, keyword-based filtering, instant alerts, and daily digests. 

# Target Users and Use Cases 

Primary Users: Small business owners exploring public contracts, bid consultants, trade associations, startups. 

Key Use Cases: 
1. A local builder sets up ‘construction, refurbishment’ alerts and gets daily emails with summarised tenders. 
2. A consultant sets multiple keyword groups for different clients and exports summaries to Excel/Google Sheets. 
3. A trade association subscribes to aggregate feeds to share weekly summaries with members.

# Key Features 

- Data ingestion: Connect to Find a Tender and Contracts Finder APIs. Pull new notices hourly and store metadata. 
- AI summarisation: Use LLMs to summarise tender titles and descriptions into 2–3 bullet points, tagged with industry categories. 
- Keyword & category matching: Users define keyword sets or select from pre-made industry categories. – We will use a chatbot interface for this 
- Automated alerts: Email and in-app notifications sent daily or instantly with short summaries and direct links. 
- Dashboard: Minimalist dashboard showing latest matches, saved filters, and alert logs. 
- User onboarding & billing: Stripe integration for free trial, monthly subscriptions, and tier upgrades.
